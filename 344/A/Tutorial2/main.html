General

  • To reset the scene, press i

Rotation

  • To rotate the cube by Y, press 1
  • To rotate the cube by X, press 2
  • To reverse the rotation direction, press R

Translation

  • To translate the cube, click and drag.

Scaling

  • To scale the cube by X, press X
  • To scale the cube by Y, press Y
  • To scale the cube by Z, press Z

Shearing

  • To shear the pyramid by X, hold A
  • To shear the pyramid by Y, hold B
  • To shear the pyramid by Z, hold C

House

  • To transform the objects into a house, press H
  • To flip the house, press F
  • All transformations (translations, rotations, scaling and shearing) should work as described above
